Listening to Music and Discussion

  • I host a radio show on the college radio station where my cohost and I invite friends to discuss and analyze music. What if listeners could be in on the conversation?
  • The application will provide a link to listen to the radio station on every page and a forum for listeners to post their comments and discuss the music being played. Any number of listeners can take part.
  • Any number of listeners can take part. All they have to do is enjoy music.
  • Listeners can also view archives of past discussions.
  • An admin may have extra tools for removing negative comments.
